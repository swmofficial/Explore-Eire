covered by DataSheet, making it unclickable. Playwright's `locator.click()` timed
out with a message about a `<div>` from the DataSheet subtree intercepting pointer
events.

## Task

Investigate whether DataSheet outer wrapper is genuinely blocking BottomNav clicks,
and fix the z-index or pointer-events layering if confirmed.

## Resolution
Verdict: PHANTOM
Commit: N/A
